Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

Welcome to the personal portfolio website of Savitha Krishnamoorthy, accessible at savitha-portfolio-sand.vercel.app. I am committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information I collect, how I use it, and your rights under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA 2023).

What Data I Collect

When you use the contact form on this website, I collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Message content

No other personal data is collected. I do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools on this website.

How I Collect Your Data

Your data is collected only when you voluntarily fill in and submit the contact form on this website. This website also uses Google reCAPTCHA v2 to protect the contact form from automated spam. Google reCAPTCHA is subject to Google's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Why I Collect Your Data

Your data is collected solely for the purpose of:

  • Responding to your inquiry, message, or request
  • Facilitating professional communication regarding HR consulting, leadership diagnostics, or speaking engagements

Your data is never used for marketing, profiling, or any purpose beyond responding to your message.

How Your Data is Stored

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a cloud database platform hosted on AWS infrastructure in the Southeast Asia (Singapore) region. Supabase implements industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Your data is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and is not kept beyond that purpose.

Sharing of Your Data

Your personal data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third party, advertiser, or external organisation. The only exception is Google reCAPTCHA, which processes data as part of its spam prevention service under Google's own privacy policy.

Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data I hold about you
  • Correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Erasure of your personal data
  • Grievance redressal if you believe your data has been mishandled

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me directly using the details in Section 8.
